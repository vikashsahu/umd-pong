Meet Our Team

What We're About

The UMCP Table Tennis (or Ping Pong) Club is inclusive to anyone who loves the game. We like to goof off, compete, teach each other, and just have a good time in general.

But I'm Not That Good...?

That's irrelevant to us. We'll help you learn, and are pretty laid back about our practices. Feel free to come play, or just watch if you'd prefer. If you do want to compete, some of our regular club members can give you a challenge.

When Do You Meet?

Tuesdays and Thursdays from 8-10 PM in the Eppley (ERC) Multipurpose room (adjacent to the kickboxing studio). Eppley usually has at least 3 tables set up during the Gym's regular hours of operation anyway, but you'll generally only see our team on T/Th.
