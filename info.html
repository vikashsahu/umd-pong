FAQS

  • What skill levels are welcome?
  • All skill levels, from beginner to advanced.
  • What if I don't have a racket?
  • Loaner rackets are available at equipment issue (ERC Basement).
  • How much are the club dues?
  • There are no club dues for the 2014-2015 schoolyear.
  • How much of a commitment is this?
  • No commitment at all. Come as often or as little as you like.
  • How can I join the club?
  • Sign the waiver located here and bring it to practice.
