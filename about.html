About the Club

The University of Maryland Table Tennis Club is an inclusive student organization recognized by the University's Campus Recreation Services that brings together students and faculty through the Olympic sport of table tennis. All skill levels are welcome, from beginner to advanced.

At least once per semester, members of the club have the option to compete in tournaments as part of the National Collegiate Table Tennis Association (NCTTA) on three levels: Divisional, Regional, and National.
